About TorMovies

TorMovies or Torrent Movies was created by a group of Cinephiles with a goal to create a platform where we can easily search, access, and download high-definition movies for free wherever & whenever we want but with less accountability. On July 2, 2021, TorMovies was made public and promoted, free for anyone to use anytime and anywhere.
